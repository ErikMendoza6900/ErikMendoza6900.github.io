Meet Compton's Dr.

Dr. D R E

Biography

  • Andre Romelle Young, also known as Dr. Dre, was born February 18,1965. He grew up in the tough streets of Compton, California attending Centennial and Fremont Highschool. His mother was a singer in a group called Four Aces, and his father was part of a band called the Romells. He developed his rap persona through Dj-ing in LA. He was part of a crew called the World Class Wreckin' Cru and eventually formed a new group with new friends called "N.W.A." which was considered the start of Ganster Rap. The Group's second album "Straight Outta Compton" sold more than 2 million copies. He is considered a legendary producer, performer, and business man.

Contributions to Society

  • Dr. Dre has impacted society in many ways that will be remembered by many generations. Aside from being apart of succesful rap group NWA, he has also helped boosts many other artist.s careers.

    Some of those artists are: Snoop Dogg, Tupac Shakur, 50 cent, Eminem, The Game, Kendrick Lamar, and many more.

    You may have herd some songs he's worked on, such as: Dr Dre - Keep their Heads Ringin; 50 Cent - In Da Club; Tupac - California Love; Snoop Dogg - Gin & Juice;

    Dr. Dre's contributions didnt stop there. After selling his Beats By Dre© company for $3 Billion dollars, boosting his net worth to $800 million, he's funded $10,000,000.00. He is planning to build a Compton Arts Center that is expected to be done by 2020. This will include a 1,200-seat theater intended to be designed as a digital media production class. As stated by Dr. Dre, he said, "My goal is to provide kids with the kind of tools and learning they deserve. The performing arts center will be a place for young people to be creative in a way that will help further their education and positively define their future."
